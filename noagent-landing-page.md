## SECTION 3 — THE PROBLEM (Why NoAgent Exists)

**Section label:** The Problem

**Headline:**
### Nigerian Agents Are Charging Too Much

**Body:**
In Nigeria, renting a home shouldn't cost you twice the rent. But that's exactly what happens. Agents stack agency fees, inspection fees, and consultation fees on top of your rent until the total amount you pay upfront nearly doubles what the landlord actually charges.

**Stat cards (3 across):**
- Card 1: "Up to 100%" — Of annual rent lost to agent fees
- Card 2: "3–5 fees" — Stacked charges per rental transaction
- Card 3: "₦0" — What NoAgent charges tenants to find a home

**Visual:** A simple before/after cost comparison
- WITHOUT NoAgent: Rent ₦500,000 + Agent fee ₦150,000 + Inspection fee ₦20,000 + Consultation ₦30,000 = ₦700,000
- WITH NoAgent: Rent ₦500,000. That's it.

## SECTION 4 — HOW IT WORKS

**Section label:** How It Works

**Headline:**
### Simple. Direct. Done.

**Steps (alternating left-right layout on desktop, stacked on mobile):**

Step 1 — Sign Up & Choose Your Role
Create your account and tell us who you are — a landlord with a property to fill, or a tenant looking for a home. Your role determines your entire experience.

Step 2 — Landlords List. Tenants Search.
Landlords post their property with photos, price, and location in minutes. Tenants search by area, budget, and bedroom count and browse verified listings instantly.

Step 3 — Connect Directly
Found a listing you love? Message the landlord directly inside the app. No phone numbers needed. Schedule an inspection, ask questions, and negotiate — all in one place.

Step 4 — Pay, Sign & Move In
Choose how long you want to stay, pay securely via Paystack, and receive your digital tenancy agreement immediately. No agent. No extra fees. Just your new home.

**Section label:** Trust & Safety

**Headline:**
### We Replaced the Agent — Not the Trust

**Body:**
The one thing agents were supposed to provide was a layer of trust between strangers. NoAgent builds that trust directly into the platform so you never have to compromise safety to save money.

Pillar 2 — Ratings & Reviews

Every tenancy on NoAgent is backed by a structured digital agreement — automatically generated, timestamped, and stored securely for both parties.
